Research

[MSc Thesis] Molecular Dynamics Simulations Study of Effect of Plasticizers on Ion Transport in Polyethylene oxide (PEO) – LiTFSI electrolytes for Li-ion Batteries

Performed MD simulation on SPE (i.e. poly(ethylene oxide)) consisting of LiTFSI salt and SN plasticisers.

From the simulated trajectories we try to calculate the ion diffusivity and explain the ion transport mechanisms using ion-pairing and polymer segmental motion of PEO chains.
